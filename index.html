GENERATIVE CROCHET

Crochet is an old craft with a rich history and a global reach. As it grew in popularity in the West, it became associated with domesticity and femininity. Because of this, its forms and applications have been constrained in the domestic world.

As part of my MFA research, I am using generative and computational design techniques to explore crochet forms, experiment with materials, and propose new applications in the product design space. In using these techniques, I am hoping to extend the reach of crochet and show designers that crochet is a viable inspiration and to show crocheters just how powerful this method of making is.
